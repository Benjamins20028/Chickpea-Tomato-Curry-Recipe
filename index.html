Chickpea Tomato Curry

Ingredients:

  • 1 tablespoon coconut oil
  • 2 large garlic cloves, finely chopped
  • 1 can of sundried tomatoes
  • 1 tablespoon fresh ginger, finely chopped
  • Spices
    • ½ tablespoon of garam masala
    • ½ tablespoon ground cumin
    • ½ tablespoon ground coriander
    • ½ tablespoon ground turmeric
    • ¼ teaspoon red chili flakes
  • tomato paste
  • broth
  • coconut milk
  • chickpeas
  • spinach

Steps To Cook:

  1. Sauté the aromatics: In a large pan, heat oil over medium heat. Add onion and cook until soft and golden (5–7 minutes).

  2. Add garlic and spices: Stir in garlic, ginger, curry powder, cumin, turmeric, and chili flakes. Cook 30–60 seconds until fragrant.

  3. Simmer the sauce:Add diced tomatoes (with juice) and stir. Let simmer for 5 minutes to thicken slightly.

  4. Add chickpeas and coconut milk:Stir in chickpeas and coconut milk. Season with salt and pepper. Simmer 10–15 minutes, stirring occasionally, until the sauce is rich and creamy.

  5. Taste and finish: Adjust seasoning. Add a squeeze of lemon or lime for brightness.

  6. Serve: Spoon over basmati rice or with naan bread, and top with chopped cilantro.
